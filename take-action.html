Take Action

Help to Elect Gun Sense Candidates This Fall

Everytown for Gun Safety has outlined which candidates, in all levels of government, will fight for gun safety legislation that will save lives in our communities and country as a whole. It is imperative that we encourage everyone who is registered to show up on election day, and sending personal postcards has been shown to increase voter turnout.

Action Checklist

  • Step 1: Visit Everytown to find your gun sense candidates
  • Step 2: Join your local Students or Moms Demand Action chapter
  • Step 3: Ask to help send postcards to voters (if from BWHS, contact me)
  • Step 4: (BWHS only) join the remind by texting @falconsto to 81010
